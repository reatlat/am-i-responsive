Take a look at how your website is responsive

Features

  • homehttp://localhost/ works so it's great for taking screenshots of local development URLs
  • shareSend someone a link with your site already embeded by using ?url=http://www.yoursite.com - check example
  • shuffleRearrange and reorder the devices by clicking and dragging on the device
  • bookmarkYou can check the responsiveness on any site with a single click. Simply drag to your bookmarks bar the red plus-buttonadd

How to use it

  • filter_1Add your URL to the input field
  • filter_2Press Enter or stop typing and just wait 1 second
  • filter_3Admire your great work

Viewports

Desktop 1600x992px scaled down to scale(0.3181)
Laptop 1280x802px scaled down to scale(0.277)
Tablet 768x1024px scaled down to scale(0.219)
Mobile 320x480px scaled down to scale(0.219)
